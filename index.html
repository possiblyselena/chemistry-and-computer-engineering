Printed Circuit Boards

PCBs are flat boards made up of layers of insulation and conductive materials to make circuits and electronics compact and efficient. These insulating materials are usually made of fiber glass or expoxy and the conductive layers are copper. The copper layers are etched to make routes to make connections in the circuit.
